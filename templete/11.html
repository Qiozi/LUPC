ABOUT ME

LU Computers is an established company providing computers and parts to home users and businesses in the US and Canada.  Based in Toronto and New York, we aim at delivering fast and courteous service to our customers.

With our hard working, dedication and efficient operation, our sales volume has been increasing tremendously year after year, which permits us to establish ties with manufacturers and negotiate prices with vendors. This price benefit is passed right on to you, our customers.
